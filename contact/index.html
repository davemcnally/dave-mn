Hi! I’m Dave McNally

I’m a freelance designer and front-end developer. It’s nice to meet you.

Assuming you’re here to learn more about me and my work, let’s get down to it. I am completely self-taught with no formal design training, having picked things up over the years I’ve been making websites. I’m constantly pushing myself to learn new things and improve upon what I already know.

Clean interfaces, mobile & performance are current passions of mine. JavaScript is my main focus for improvement at the moment. I know enough to get by with most front-end tasks but I'm no programmer. HTML & CSS are old friends of mine. My development toolkit typically consists of Sass, Grunt, Git and Jekyll with Photoshop and Sketch covering design requirements. I like a mobile-first approach to development but with a focus on design at all sizes — design for the content, not for the screen size.
